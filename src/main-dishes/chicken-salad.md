Emilie's Chicken Salad

3 cans chicken
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tablespoon dijon mustard
1/2 cup red grapes, quartered
1 stalk celery, diced
1-2 green onions (green and white parts), sliced
1/2 lemon, juiced
salt and pepper, to taste

Makes 6 sandwiches.
